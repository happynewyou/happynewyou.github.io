Happy New You - Activities☰

Mentioned are Signature and Buffer Activities centers can take up...

Group Meditation

You can contact a Heartfulness Trainer and arrange Group Meditation sessions at 7:30 AM and 5:00 PM during the event.

Tree Plantation

Let's Spread the 'Go Green' Initiative through tree plantation in Ashrams. You can also connect with an NGO or with NSS students to plan a large scale event in your city.

Also Sponsor a sapling/tree/group of trees at Kanha be a part of the Green Kanha Movement! Visit https://kanha.sahajmarg.org/green-kanha for more Info.

Heartfulness Yoga

The main objective of Heartfulness Yoga classes is to introduce people as students and then train them as teachers not only to classical yoga asanas but to the ultimate goal of Yoga, which is oneness through meditation.

Get in touch with one of Heartfulness Yoga Trainers and organize a session. Feel the Joy of YOGA

Individual Sittings

An Individual Sitting with a Heartfulness Trainer can help you deepen your sadhana.

Ask trainers at your ashram to be available at a specific time for abhyasis in need of guidance.

Exchanging Words of Compassion

Let us Exchange letters with a few words of love or kindness or compassion between centers. These can be cards/letters/bookmarks with a few hand written words,signed with the First Name and City of the writer.

Webinar

A live talk is planned on the 29th December at 11:00 AM, wherein Brother Rishi Ranjan will be joining us. Click the link to join... https://zoom.us/j/529211927

Giving back to community

We can invite visitors from an Old Age Home to our Ashram. Our special guests can join us for the evening satsang wherein we can introduce them to HFN. We also can go to Old age Home or Orphanage, and spend time with them.

Decoration

In the spirit of Christmas, we can plan an activity where we can choose trees within the ashram premises, ashram building/ halls or public parks (public spaces with appropriate permission from authorities) as the venue and Decorate the same.

Spreading Joy in Hospitals

We can tie up with a hospital wing or floor. We can host a painting activity with children at Ashram. The paintings can then be framed and gifted to the hospital wing or floor for decorating their walls.

Santa...!!!

Each center can have a brother or sister dressed as Santa till the end of decoration competition. Our Santa will ask questions and upon receiving satisfactory answers, will give decorating items as gifts
