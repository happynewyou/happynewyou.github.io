About Happy New You

Christmas is all about giving. We, at Heartfulness Institute have so much to offer. Let us not limit ourselves just to gifts. A simple act of kindness goes a long way.

Introducing Heartfulness to our brothers and sisters or at the least making them aware of our presence can be our own special way of celebrating Christmas and welcoming 2019!!!

The Happy New You event is happening across the globe in 20 Indian and 5 overseas cities on the 29th and 30th December, 2018.

You can get in touch with us at yearoftheheart@gmail.com
